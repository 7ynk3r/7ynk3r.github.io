# Bookshelf

A collection of books that have shaped my thinking about leadership, technology,
and personal development.

## Leadership & Management

- **The 48 Laws of Power** by Robert Greene
- **The Making of a Manager** by Julie Zhuo
- **The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People** by Stephen R. Covey

## Psychology & Human Behavior

- **Surrounded by Idiots** by Thomas Erikson
- **Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion** by Robert B. Cialdini
- **Thinking, Fast and Slow** by Daniel Kahneman

## Personal Development

- **Meditations** by Marcus Aurelius
- **Atomic Habits** by James Clear
- **The Art of Seduction** by Robert Greene
- **The Art of War** by Sun Tzu

## Biographies

- **Elon Musk** by Walter Isaacson
- **The Snowball: Warren Buffett and the Business of Life** by Alice Schroeder
- **Steve Jobs** by Walter Isaacson
- **Genghis Khan and the Making of the Modern World** by Jack Weatherford

## Literature & Philosophy

- **1984** by George Orwell
- **Demian** by Hermann Hesse

---

_This list represents books that have influenced my perspective on engineering
leadership, technology, and personal growth. Each has contributed to my
understanding of how to build better teams and systems._
